## Wilkesboro.net Research Project

### Project Overview
Building a comprehensive community resource directory for Wilkesboro.net covering Wilkes County and surrounding counties in northwestern North Carolina.

### Counties Covered
- Wilkes County (primary)
- Caldwell County
- Alexander County
- Iredell County
- Alleghany County
- Ashe County
- Surry County
- Yadkin County
- Watauga County

### Key Resources Discovered

#### Critical Housing Resources
- **Catherine H. Barber Memorial Shelter** (Wilkes) - Only homeless shelter in Wilkes County, operating since 1987
- **Hospitality House of NWNC** - Regional shelter serving 7 counties
- **Shelter Home of Caldwell County** - DV and homeless services
- **Alexander County Emergency Shelter** - County-operated emergency shelter

#### Healthcare
- **Wilkes Medical Center** - Wake Forest Baptist Health
- **UNC Health Caldwell** - Lenoir hospital
- **Iredell Health System** - Statesville and Mooresville
- **Iredell Urgent Care Mooresville** - Only 24-hour non-hospital urgent care in region

#### Employment
- **NCWorks Career Centers** - Comprehensive employment services in all counties
- Special programs for veterans, youth, formerly incarcerated
