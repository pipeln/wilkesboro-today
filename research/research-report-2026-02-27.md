## NOTABLE DISCOVERIES

1. **Wilkes Medical Center** is now part of Wake Forest Baptist Health (since 2017), providing expanded resources including specialized rehabilitation and wound healing centers.

2. **Wilkes Transportation Authority** provides comprehensive public transit including on-demand service anywhere in NC - a valuable resource for medical appointments and employment.

3. **SAFE (Sheltered Aid for Families in Emergency)** provides the only dedicated domestic violence/sexual assault services in Wilkes County with 24/7 crisis response.

4. **NCWorks Career Center** offers comprehensive employment services including career planning, training, and direct job placement assistance.

5. **Wilkes Community Partnership for Children (Smart Start)** offers extensive family support including diaper assistance, child care scholarships, and parenting education.

6. **Veterans Services Office** has moved to a new location at the Wilkes County Agricultural Center (416 Executive Drive Suite F).

7. **Daymark Recovery Services** is the primary mental health/substance abuse provider in the county, offering MAT (Medication-Assisted Treatment) for opioid use disorder.

8. **Samaritan Kitchen** serves approximately 100 families weekly and provides backpack meals to 600+ children.
