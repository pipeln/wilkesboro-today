## Notable Discoveries:

2. **Catherine H. Barber Shelter** is Wilkes County's ONLY homeless shelter
3. **Fifth Street Ministries** in Iredell is a regional model - 150 beds, 64,000+ meals

5. **Legal Aid of NC** serves all counties with free civil legal help

## Gaps Identified:
- Alexander County needs emergency shelter
- Limited mental health inpatient beds regionally
- Transportation barriers in rural areas
- Need for more free/low-cost dental services
