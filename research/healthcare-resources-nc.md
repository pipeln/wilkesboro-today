## RESEARCH NOTES

### Search Strategy:
1. "[County] NC hospital" for primary hospitals
2. "[County] NC urgent care" for walk-in clinics
3. "FQHC [County] NC" for community health centers
4. Cross-referenced with health system websites (Wake Forest, UNC, Iredell)

### Key Findings:
- Wilkes Medical Center is the primary hospital for Wilkes County
- Caldwell County has strong UNC Health presence with multiple urgent care locations
- Iredell County has two major health systems (Iredell Health, Duke Lake Norman)
- Alexander County has NO hospital - residents travel to Iredell or Catawba counties
- Urgent Care of Mountain View is building new facility in Taylorsville

### Healthcare Gaps Identified:
- Alexander County lacks hospital/emergency room
- Limited psychiatric inpatient beds in region
- Rural areas may have transportation barriers to care
