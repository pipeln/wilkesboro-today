## NOTABLE DISCOVERIES

1. **Wilkes Medical Center** is now part of the Atrium Health Wake Forest Baptist system, providing access to world-class healthcare resources

2. **Samaritan Kitchen of Wilkes** is a major food assistance provider, distributing over 772,000 pounds of food annually to nearly 10,000 households

3. **Catherine H. Barber Memorial Shelter** is the ONLY homeless shelter in Wilkes County, operating 365 days a year since 1987

4. **Wilkes Transportation Authority** provides the only scheduled public transportation in Wilkes County with the WE Shuttle running Monday, Wednesday, Friday

5. **Daymark Recovery Services** provides 24/7 crisis mental health services in Wilkes County

6. **North Wilkesboro Comprehensive Treatment Center** offers medication-assisted treatment for opioid use disorder

7. **Legal Aid of North Carolina** provides statewide free legal assistance for civil matters

8. **Wilkes County lacks a dedicated domestic violence shelter** - survivors must access services from surrounding counties or call state hotlines
