## SCHOOL BOARD RACE (3 Candidates for 2 Seats)

**Note:** Non-partisan election held March 3, 2026 (same date as primaries)

### 1. Rudy Holbrook (Incumbent - Chairman)
- **Position:** Incumbent Chairman seeking re-election
- **Email:** nikkirudy@aol.com
- **Ballotpedia:** https://ballotpedia.org/Randall_Rudy_Holbrook_(Wilkes_County_Schools,_At-large,_North_Carolina,_candidate_2026)
- **Qualifications:** 46+ years as businessman, dealing with people daily
- **Campaign Themes (from Wilkes Record questionnaire):**
  - Wants kids to always be safe
  - Help prepare students for careers after graduation
  - School budget will be tough but has dealt with budgets most of his life
  - Wants to help every student successfully finish school

### 2. Jammie Jolly (Incumbent)
- **Position:** Incumbent Board Member seeking re-election
- **Ballotpedia:** https://ballotpedia.org/Jammie_Y._Jolly_(Wilkes_County_Board_of_Education,_North_Carolina,_candidate_2022)
- **Facebook:** https://www.facebook.com/p/Jammie-Y-Jolly-Wilkes-County-School-Board-100075913638841/
- **Qualifications:**
  - 32.4 years in public education
  - 18 years as classroom teacher
  - 10 years as instructional specialist in Wilkes County Schools
  - 4 years as Director of School Improvement for Winston-Salem/Forsyth County Schools
  - Teaching degree from Appalachian State University
  - Master's degree from East Carolina University
  - Co-owner of Jolly Bell's Education Consultants
- **Campaign Themes (from Wilkes Record questionnaire):**
  - Declining enrollment + rising costs = doing more with less
  - Must be strong stewards of taxpayer dollars
  - Prioritize spending aligned with student needs
  - Strengthen partnerships with county leaders, community organizations, businesses
  - Advocate for fair, sustainable funding for rural districts
  - Protect instructional quality and support teachers

### 3. Jose Rivera (Challenger)
- **Position:** Challenger
- **Profession:** Doctor (semi-retired physician and educator)
- **Education:** Antilles High School
- **Ballotpedia:** https://ballotpedia.org/Jose_Rivera_(Wilkes_County_Schools,_At-large,_North_Carolina,_candidate_2026)
- **Campaign Themes (from Ballotpedia survey):**
  - Platform: Enable students to fulfill God-given talent in learning
  - Encourage teachers and students to find areas of aptitude
  - Key issues: Faith, Family, Education
- **Campaign Themes (from Wilkes Record questionnaire):**
  - Concerned students don't know about current events or future plans
  - Teachers frustrated curriculum holds them back from actual teaching
  - Feels teaching is "to pass the test, not gain knowledge for life"
  - Past board member of South Carolina Minority Affairs Commission (14 years)
  - Has taught from middle school through graduate university levels
  - Main issues: Funding, curriculum, and social issues
