## ‚ö†Ô∏è NOTES ON RELIABILITY AND ACCESS

### Free vs. Paywalled Content
- **Free Access**: Mountain Xpress, Watauga Democrat (most content), AVLtoday, Carolina Public Press, Government feeds
- **Partial Paywall**: Asheville Citizen-Times (Gannett), Winston-Salem Journal (Lee), Greensboro News & Record (Lee)
- **Usually Free**: Journal-Patriot (weekly), WHKY

### Feed Reliability
- **Government feeds**: Generally reliable, updated during business hours
- **Newspaper feeds**: Vary by publisher; some use FeedBurner or similar services
- **Weather feeds**: NWS feeds are highly reliable and updated frequently
- **University feeds**: Reliable during academic year, may slow during breaks

### Technical Notes
- Most feeds are RSS 2.0 or Atom format
- Some government sites use CivicEngage platform for RSS
- NWS uses CAP (Common Alerting Protocol) for emergency alerts
- If a feed URL doesn't work, try adding `/feed/` or `/rss/` to the base domain

### Coverage Overlap
- **Wilkes County**: Journal-Patriot (primary), WHKY (secondary)
- **Watauga/Boone**: Watauga Democrat (primary), The Appalachian (campus)
- **Asheville**: Citizen-Times (daily), Mountain Xpress (weekly alt), AVLtoday (digital)
- **Winston-Salem**: Journal (daily), Chronicle (community)
- **Greensboro**: News & Record (daily), Rhino Times (weekly)
- **Hickory**: Daily Record (primary)
