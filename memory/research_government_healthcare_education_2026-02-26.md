## SUMMARY

**Resources Found:**
- Government Offices: 10 counties covered with main offices, DSS, and elected officials
- Hospitals: 11 hospitals/medical centers across all target counties
- School Districts: 9 public school districts documented
- Libraries: Multiple public library systems
- Homeless Shelters: 5+ shelters identified
- Food Assistance: 10+ food banks/pantries/kitchens
- Employment: NCWorks centers in all major counties
- Legal Aid: Statewide and local resources
- Senior Services: Multiple senior centers and Meals on Wheels
- Veteran Services: County offices and state resources

**Notable Discoveries:**
1. Catherine H. Barber Memorial Shelter is Wilkes County's ONLY homeless shelter (open since 1987)
2. Fifth Street Ministries in Iredell County serves ~150 homeless individuals
3. Appalachian Regional Library serves 3 counties (Ashe, Wilkes, Watauga)
4. Northern Regional Hospital in Surry County is being acquired by Novant Health
5. Alleghany Health is a Critical Access Hospital serving rural mountain communities
6. Most counties have NCWorks Career Centers for employment assistance

**Next Research Priorities:**
- Mental health services and crisis resources
- Substance abuse treatment centers
- Domestic violence shelters
- Child and family services
- Transportation services
- Disability services
- Utility assistance programs
