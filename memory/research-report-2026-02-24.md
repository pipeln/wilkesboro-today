## NOTABLE DISCOVERIES

1. **Wilkes Transportation Authority** operates the only scheduled public transportation in Wilkes County (WE Shuttle) - Mon/Wed/Fri, $3 fare

2. **Catherine H. Barber Memorial Shelter** is Wilkes County's ONLY homeless shelter - operating since 1987, 365 days/year

3. **Wilkes County EMS** is substantial - established 1971, 39 full-time + 20 part-time paramedics, 10,000+ calls/year, 4 ambulances + 1 QRV

4. **Daymark Recovery Services** is the primary mental health/substance abuse provider with a dedicated crisis line: 336-838-9936

5. **No VA Clinic in Wilkes County** - Veterans must travel to surrounding areas; local office helps navigate benefits

6. **Wilkes Housing and Outreach Center** is a newer resource (opened by Hospitality House) at 1904 West Park Drive

7. **Samaritan Kitchen** provides evening meals at 5pm daily and a Food Backpack Program for children

8. **Wilkes County covers 756 square miles** - third largest county in NC by area

9. **Recovery Revolution** (R3) is an active local recovery community organization with a physical center

10. **Multiple counties lack hospitals** - Alexander County's hospital closed in 2004; residents travel to Iredell, Catawba, or Caldwell for hospital care
