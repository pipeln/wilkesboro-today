## MARCH 3, 2026 PRIMARY ELECTIONS

### WILKES BOARD OF EDUCATION (Non-partisan, 2 seats)

#### Rudy Holbrook (Incumbent)
- **Position:** Current Chairman, Wilkes Board of Education
- **Experience:** 46+ years as businessman; serving as school board member
- **Platform:** Focus on student safety, career preparation for graduates, budget management
- **Key Quote:** "I want the kids to always be safe. I want to help prepare all of our kids for whatever career that they want after they graduate."
- **Main Issues:** School budget challenges, helping every student successfully finish school

#### Jammie Jolly (Incumbent)
- **Background:** Product of Wilkes County Schools, former teacher, instructional coach
- **Experience:** 32.4 years in public education (18 years classroom teacher, 10 years instructional specialist in Wilkes County, 4 years Director of School Improvement for Winston-Salem/Forsyth County Schools)
- **Education:** Teaching degree from Appalachian State University, master's from East Carolina University
- **Current Role:** Co-owner of Jolly Bell's Education Consultants
- **Platform:** Steady, thoughtful leadership; strong stewardship of taxpayer dollars; data-informed decisions; strengthening partnerships with county leaders and community organizations
- **Main Issues:** Declining enrollment, rising operational costs, protecting instructional quality, supporting teachers

#### Jose Rivera (Challenger)
- **Profession:** Doctor
- **Background:** Former board member of South Carolina Minority Affairs Commission (14 years); past educator teaching middle school through graduate university levels
- **Platform:** Addressing curriculum concerns, teacher frustrations with test-focused teaching, family engagement with schools
- **Key Quote:** "After much prayer, I was called to intervene by my Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ."
- **Main Issues:** Funding, curriculum reform, social issues, school violence

### WILKES COUNTY SHERIFF (Republican Primary, 6 candidates)

#### Chris Shew (Incumbent)
- **Current Position:** Wilkes County Sheriff (15+ years)
- **Career:** Started with Wilkes County Sheriff's Office in 1980 as part-time deputy; served in every rank including Deputy, Sergeant, Captain, Chief of Detectives, Major, Chief Deputy
- **Certifications:** Sheriff's Training and Standards Commission; Sheriff's Leadership Institute graduate (UNC Chapel Hill)
- **Other Service:** Roaring River Fire Department Board; NC Lottery Commissioner (7 years, including Vice Chair); NC Sheriffs' Association Legislative Committee
- **Key Accomplishments:**
  - Implemented FLOCK Automated License Plate Readers
  - Established Medical Assisted Treatment (MAT) program in jail
  - Launched Law Enforcement Assisted Diversion (LEAD) program
- **Main Issues:** Drug enforcement, cybercrime preparation, civil unrest preparedness, mental health challenges

#### Eric Byrd
- **Background:** Former Wilkes County Sheriff's deputy; served with Sheriff Shew

- **Platform:** Modernize where it makes sense, reduce drugs, cut theft, keep neighborhoods safe, protect children
