## NOTABLE DISCOVERIES

1. **Hurricane Helene Impact**: Multiple counties (especially Watauga, Ashe, Alleghany) are still recovering from Hurricane Helene (September 2024). Many resources are focused on disaster recovery.

2. **Food Insecurity**: Caldwell County has a 16% food insecurity rate (above national average of 13.5%). Child food insecurity in Caldwell is 20.1%.

3. **Rural Healthcare**: Alleghany County has only one hospital (Alleghany Memorial Hospital). Wilkes Medical Center joined Wake Forest Baptist Health in 2017.

4. **Veteran Services**: Multiple counties have dedicated Veterans Services offices (Wilkes, Ashe, etc.)

5. **Community Colleges**: Wilkes Community College has academic centers in Alleghany County (Sparta) and serves the region.
