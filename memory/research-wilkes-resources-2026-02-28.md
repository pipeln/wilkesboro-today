## NOTABLE DISCOVERIES

1. **Wilkes Medical Center** is a 130-bed facility with comprehensive services including dialysis, rehabilitation, and wound healing - more extensive than initially cataloged.

2. **Wilkes Senior Resources** offers extensive programming including fitness, yoga, line dancing, and congregate meals - a key senior hub.

3. **Blessing Boxes** are widespread throughout the county - over 20 locations identified at schools, churches, and community centers.

4. **Daymark Recovery Services** provides critical mental health and substance abuse services with walk-in availability.

5. **Wilkes Recovery Revolution** is a newer organization offering comprehensive recovery support including housing.

6. **Multiple Food Pantries** exist beyond Samaritan Kitchen - including church-based and community center pantries.

7. **Wilkes Transportation Authority** provides both fixed-route and on-demand services throughout NC.

8. **Veterans Services Office** has moved to the Agricultural Center at 416 Executive Drive.

9. **Wilkes Vocational Services** provides employment support for adults with disabilities.

10. **Children's Hope Alliance** (formerly Barium Springs) provides emergency shelter and residential treatment for children.
