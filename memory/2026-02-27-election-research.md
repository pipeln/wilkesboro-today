## MARCH 3, 2026 PRIMARY ELECTIONS

### WILKES BOARD OF EDUCATION (Non-partisan, 2 seats)

**Rudy Holbrook** (Incumbent, Chairman)
- **Background:** Businessman with 46+ years of experience
- **Experience:** Current Chairman of Wilkes Board of Education
- **Platform:** Student safety, career preparation for graduates, school budget management
- **Key Issues:** School budget challenges, helping every student successfully finish school
- **Quote:** "I want the kids to always be safe. I want to help prepare all of our kids for whatever career that they want after they graduate from high school."

**Jammie Jolley** (Incumbent)
- **Background:** Product of Wilkes County Schools, former teacher, instructional coach
- **Education:** Teaching degree from Appalachian State University, Master's from East Carolina University
- **Experience:** 32.4 years in public education (18 years classroom teacher, 10 years instructional specialist in Wilkes County, 4 years Director of School Improvement for Winston-Salem/Forsyth County Schools)
- **Current:** First term on Board of Education, co-owner of Jolly Bell's Education Consultants
- **Platform:** Steady, thoughtful leadership; fiscal responsibility; maintaining instructional quality
- **Key Issues:** Declining enrollment, rising operational costs, protecting teacher support and student opportunities
- **Quote:** "Experience and consistency matter. I want to continue serving the school system that has invested so much in me and my family."

**Jose Rivera** (Challenger)
- **Background:** Doctor, former educator (middle school through graduate university levels)
- **Experience:** 14 years as board member of South Carolina Minority Affairs Commission
- **Platform:** Addressing student preparedness, teacher curriculum frustrations, family engagement
- **Key Issues:** Funding, curriculum reform, social issues, school violence
- **Quote:** "After much prayer, I was called to intervene by my Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ."

### WILKES COUNTY SHERIFF (Republican Primary - 6 candidates)

**Chris Shew** (Incumbent)
- **Background:** 63 years old, Antioch community
- **Experience:** Serving as Sheriff for 15 years (3rd term); served in every rank at Sheriff's Office (Deputy, Sergeant, Captain, Chief of Detectives, Major, Chief Deputy); certified by Sheriff's Training and Standards Commission; graduate of Sheriff's Leadership Institute at UNC Chapel Hill
- **Additional Roles:** Roaring River Fire Department Board of Directors; NC Lottery Commissioner (7 years, including Vice Chair); NC Sheriffs' Association Legislative Committee; Line of Duty Death Committee
- **Key Issues:** Drug enforcement, cybercrime, civil unrest preparedness, mental health challenges; implemented FLOCK License Plate Readers, Medical Assisted Treatment (MAT) program in jail, Law Enforcement Assisted Diversion (LEAD) program
- **Quote:** "I have dedicated my entire life to public safety, beginning at age 18 as a volunteer firefighter."

**Eric Byrd** (Challenger)
- **Background:** 52 years old, Moravian Falls community
- **Experience:** Over 30 years as certified deputy sheriff; served in every division of Sheriff's Office; Training and Supervising Officer for over a decade; SWAT Operator (14 years); FBI Certified Hazardous Devices Technician (bomb technician); ATF Certified Post Blast Investigator; US Department of Defense Contractor in Afghanistan embedded with US Army
- **Certifications:** NC Justice Academy Certified Field Training Officer, Criminal Investigator, General Instructor, Specialized Explosives Instructor, Firearms Instructor, Concealed Carry Handgun Instructor; NRA Certified Firearms Instructor
- **Key Issues:** Modernizing Sheriff's Office, reducing drugs, cutting theft, neighborhood safety, protecting children
- **Quote:** "I've spent more than 30 years serving in every division of the Sheriff's Office, rolling up my sleeves and doing the work."

**David Carson** (Challenger)
- **Background:** Former Wilkes County Sheriff's deputy
- **Note:** Served with Sheriff Shew previously

**James Dowell** (Challenger)
- **Background:** Former Wilkes County Sheriff's deputy
- **Note:** Served with Sheriff Shew previously

**David Gambill Jr.** (Challenger)
- **Background:** Former Wilkes County Sheriff's deputy
- **Note:** Served with Sheriff Shew previously

**Sharon Call-Diaz** (Challenger)
