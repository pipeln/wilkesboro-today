## NOTABLE DISCOVERIES

1. **Wilkes Medical Center** is now part of Wake Forest Baptist Health (since 2017), providing academic medical center resources locally

2. **Wilkes Community Health Center** is a Federally Qualified Health Center (FQHC) offering sliding fee scales and serving all patients regardless of ability to pay

3. **Catherine H. Barber Memorial Shelter** is Wilkes County's ONLY homeless shelter, operating 365 days/year since 1987

4. **Samaritan Kitchen of Wilkes** distributed 772,677 pounds of food to 9,737 households in recent reporting period

5. **Wilkes County EMS** responds to over 12,000 calls annually across 756 square miles (3rd largest county in NC)

6. **Legal Aid of North Carolina** provides statewide coverage with Winston-Salem office serving the Wilkes region

7. **Hospitality House of NWNC** serves a 7-county region including Wilkes (Alleghany, Ashe, Avery, Mitchell, Watauga, Wilkes, Yancey)

8. **Wilkes Communications** is a locally-owned fiber provider with innovative rural broadband deployment
